Frequently Asked Questions

Is SmashUTME free to use? expand_more
SmashUTME offers a comprehensive free tier that includes foundational practice exams and core study modules. For students seeking advanced diagnostic tools and full simulated mock exams, we offer a premium Precision Plan.
How do I get started? expand_more
Simply click the "Get Started" button in the navigation bar. You'll create a clinical profile where you can select your target subjects and set your UTME goal score.
Will more subjects be added? expand_more
Yes, our engineering team is constantly calibrating new subject modules. We follow the official JAMB syllabus strictly to ensure clinical accuracy in all our practice material.
