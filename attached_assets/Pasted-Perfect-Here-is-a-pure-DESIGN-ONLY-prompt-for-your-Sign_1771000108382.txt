# 📝 SIGNUP PAGE – DESIGN SPECIFICATION

## Emotional Goal

The student should feel:

* Calm
* Encouraged
* Ready to start
* Not overwhelmed

---

## Layout

### Top Section

* Centered SmashUTME logo
* Short tagline:
  “Prepare smarter. Smash UTME.”
* Small Badge component:
  “Free for 2026 Candidates”

Keep top area clean with generous spacing.

---

### Main Section (Centered Card Component)

Card should:

* Be vertically centered on desktop
* Have soft rounded corners
* Subtle shadow
* Adequate padding
* Max-width optimized for mobile

Inside the Card:

Form Fields (stacked vertically):

* Full Name
* Email
* Password (with eye toggle icon)
* Confirm Password

Spacing:

* Clear vertical rhythm
* Large touch-friendly inputs

Below fields:

* Small checkbox:
  “I am preparing for UTME 2026”

Primary Button:

* Full-width
* Rounded
* Solid primary color (calm blue or green)
* Label: “Create Account”

Divider:

* Horizontal line
* Centered text “or”

Secondary Button:

* Outline style
* “Continue with Google”

Bottom Text:

* Small link:
  “Already have an account? Login”

---

## Visual Style

* Light background (very light gray or soft gradient)
* Primary color: calm blue or green
* Typography:

  * Clean sans-serif
  * Clear hierarchy
  * Medium-weight headings
* Large input fields
* No background illustrations
* No busy graphics

“Select your UTME subjects”

“Choose the subjects you are writing.”

* Clean selectable cards OR
* Large checkboxes in 2-column grid (mobile collapses to 1)

* Slightly highlighted

“Continue”

Subtle text button

Design Feel:

* Spacious
* Clear
